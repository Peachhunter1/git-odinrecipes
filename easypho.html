Easy Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup dish consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs and meat. Pho is a popular food in Vietnam where it is served in households, street stalls and restaurants countrywide. Pho is considered Vietnam's national dish.

Ingredients

  • vermicelli
  • ribeye beef (sukiyaki)
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • 1 box pho broth
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • red pepper
  • bok choy
  • cilantro
  • lime

Steps

  1. Begin by cutting your garlic and giner. Add to a pan with oil on medium heat. Simmer until fragrant but not burned.
  2. Add in your choice of beef and cook to the level of your choice. Meanwhile prepare the broth by adding 1 box of pho broth to a pot, spice up the broth with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, while that boils cooks your vermicelli. It should only take 2-4 minutes as they cook very quickly. Once cooked, place the noodles in a bowl, spoon your broth in, add your beef and whatever vegetables you prefer.
  4. Top with cilantro, lime, and sweet chili sauce. Enjoy!
