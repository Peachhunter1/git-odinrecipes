Greek Lemon Rice Soup

Description

Ahvo-lemono, as the Greeks pronounce it, are one of Greek's widely-known traditional dishes that everyone loves. It is a silky, rich, fragrant chicken soup, prepared Greek-style with avgolemono sauce(lemon-egg sauce).

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Chicken Stock
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2cup rice
  • 1 large chicken breast shredded
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 lemon juice
  • dill and lemon zest
  • salt
  • pepper
  • onion powder
  • garlic powder
  • greek seasoning

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken with listed seasoning for a few minutes on each side. Remove and let rest for 10 minutes before shredding.
  2. Meanwhile add your chicken broth, garlic, and rice to a pot. Bring to a boil and then simmer on low until rice is cooked through. Season the broth your liking.
  3. Add in shredded chicekn. Mix the eggs and lemon juice together separately. Slowly add the mixture to the pot a bit at a time while stirring quickly. Make sure it is not boiling when pouring in the eggs, otherwise the eggs will cook.
  4. Add any other seasonings at this point. Your soup should be creamy and think. Top with dill and lemon zest and enjoy!
