Cheesy Beef Pasta

Cheesy Beef Pasta are well known for its other name, Cheeseburger Pasta It's a rich, creamy, and comforting pasta dish. Packed with two knds of cheese, savory ground beef, and tender pasta. It all comes together in just one skillet and packs a powerful punch for taste!

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves
  • lean ground beef
  • 1/3 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 cups milk
  • 4 cups pasta
  • Shredded Cheddar
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  1. Dice your oion and mince your garlic. Add onion to a pot with hot oil. Simmer for a couple minutes until clear. Add in your beef and cook until almost no pink remains.
  2. Add in seasonings, garlic, and tomato sauce. Mix well, add in beef broth and milk. Bring to a boil and add in pasta. Cook until pasta is tender and liquid is almost gone.
  3. Add in shredded cheese of heat and mix well. Serve with parsley and more pepper flakes.
